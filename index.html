About me

I am a researcher in computer science in the area of formal methods. I am currently doing my Ph.D. at CISPA Helmholtz Center for Information Security (Germany) supervise by Rayna Dimitrova. I focus on practical algorithms and tools for synthesizing infinite-state systems.
